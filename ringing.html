Bay Area Guild of Change Ringers

We are the Bay Area Guild of Change Ringers, founded in 2013. There are no towers in the area, but we ring handbells. We ring on a set of eight bells graciously loaned to us by Laura Dickerson. The regulars are Edward, Risha and Jenna. Former regulars include Leland.

We're currently on hiatus because one of our regulars has a hand injury, so we don't have enough ringers. If you are planning a visit, please do let us know by email so we can setup a practice. We are not too advanced: we are working on Kent and a quarter of Plain Bob Minor, but we would love to have your presence, beginner or advanced.
