Bay Area Guild of Change Ringers

We are the Bay Area Guild of Change Ringers, founded in 2013. There are no towers in the area, but we ring handbells Thursday nights from 10pm-midnight at the Gates Computer Science Building on Stanford campus. We ring on a set of eight bells graciously loaned to us by Laura Dickerson. The regulars are Edward, Risha and Jenna. Former regulars include Leland.

If you are planning a visit, please do let us know by email! (In particular, you will need one of our phone numbers so we can let you into the building.) If you think you can't make Thursday night, let us know; we are often known to reschedule to other times (just look at the calendar history). We are not too advanced: we are working on Kent and a quarter of Plain Bob Minor, but we would love to have your presence, beginner or advanced.
